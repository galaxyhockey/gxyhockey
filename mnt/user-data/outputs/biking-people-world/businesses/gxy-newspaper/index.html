Biking People World Enters New Era of Expansion

Biking People World, the collaborative Minecraft server home to GalaxyHockey, Thenew7ihave, and Tree_Shork, is entering what insiders are calling a golden age of development. New builds are underway across both The City and MiniGame Land, with businesses reporting record activity and community engagement at an all-time high.

Sources close to city planning confirm that Market Avenue Apartments has reached full capacity, while The City Hotel has been welcoming a steady stream of visitors. Meanwhile, across the map, MiniGame Land's Dave n' Busters has seen lines around the block every weekend since its grand opening.
