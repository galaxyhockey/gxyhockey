About the World

🌍

The World

Biking People World is a collaborative Minecraft server where players build cities, run businesses, and shape the economy and culture of a living world.

🏙

Two Cities

The world is home to two distinct cities — The City, the bustling main metropolis, and MiniGame Land, the entertainment hub.

📰

Economy

Real player-run businesses operate across the world, from newspapers to hotels, cinemas, and entertainment venues.

🎮

Community

Three founding members — GalaxyHockey, Thenew7ihave, and Tree_Shork — keep the world alive and growing every day.

World History

Chapter 1 — The Beginning

World Founded

GalaxyHockey, Thenew7ihave, and Tree_Shork join forces to create Biking People World, setting out to build something unlike any other Minecraft world.

Chapter 2 — Urban Expansion

The City Is Built

The first major city takes shape. Roads, buildings, and infrastructure are laid down as The City begins to grow into a real metropolis.

Chapter 3 — Industry

Businesses Open Their Doors

GXY Newspaper publishes its first edition. The City Hotel opens. Market Avenue Apartments welcome their first residents. Commerce begins.

Chapter 4 — Entertainment

MiniGame Land Founded

The second city, MiniGame Land, is established as the entertainment capital of the world. Dave n' Busters opens its doors to players from across the server.

Chapter 5 — Present Day

The World Thrives

Biking People World continues to grow, with new builds, events, and stories written every time a player logs on.
