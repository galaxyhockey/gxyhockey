Breaking News

A new Hide and Seek map is officially being planned for MiniGame Land, and early details suggest it may become the hardest map ever added to the game.

The upcoming map is planned to be rated Extreme Difficulty. Officials have confirmed that the map is expected to feature a 20-minute timer, making it much longer than the current Hide and Seek maps.

At this time, there is no official word on what the map will be, where it will be located, or what theme it will use.

No Map Theme Revealed Yet

MiniGame Land has not revealed the name or setting of the new map. The mystery has already started rumors across the park — some players believe the map could be connected to the Nether, Piglin Plaza, Skyline Street, the Moon, or even an abandoned city-style location.

Possible Rumored Themes

  • Nether Fortress
  • Abandoned Mall
  • Moon Base
  • Mega City
  • Haunted Hotel
  • Factory District
  • Deep Cave System

None of these have been confirmed.

20-Minute Timer Could Change Everything

A 20-minute Hide and Seek match would be a major change from the current maps. The longer timer could allow for:

What the Longer Timer Could Mean

  • Larger hiding areas
  • More complex map design
  • Harder seeker gameplay
  • Longer survival strategies
  • More intense final minutes
  • Bigger rewards or leaderboard value

If the map is truly rated Extreme, players should expect difficult hiding spots, dangerous routes, and a much more serious challenge than Cave or Japanese Village.

Announcement Coming June 26

More information is expected on June 26, 2026 — the same day as the full MiniGame Land Expansion Project details announcement. This means the new Extreme Hide and Seek map may be part of the larger expansion reveal from the MiniGame Land Expansion Project Commission, also known as MGLEPC.

The GXY Newspaper will continue following this story as more details become available.

Public Notice

Players should prepare for a major Hide and Seek update. The new Extreme map is not open yet, but an official announcement is planned for June 26. Until then, the map name, theme, layout, and release date remain unknown.
