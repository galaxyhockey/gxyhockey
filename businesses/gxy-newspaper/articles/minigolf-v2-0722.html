Mini Golf v2 Preview Revealed

New ball physics, a massive shop overhaul, career tracking, tournaments, and practice mode — MGLEPC previews the biggest Mini Golf update yet.

By the GXY Reporting Team · Mini Golf · Wednesday, July 22, 2026

After the success of the Mini Golf open beta and launch, MGLEPC has revealed the first preview of Mini Golf v2. The update will bring major improvements and new features, transforming the game from a simple attraction into a full competitive experience.

New Ball Physics

Mini Golf v2 will introduce improved ball movement, making shots feel more realistic and skill-based. Players can expect better:

Physics Improvements

  • Movement control
  • Collisions
  • Rolling physics
  • Course interaction

Massive Shop Overhaul

The Mini Golf shop is receiving a major redesign. The updated shop may include:

In the New Shop

  • New cosmetics
  • Unlockable items
  • Customization options
  • Rewards
  • Progression items

Career Tracking

A new career system is coming to Mini Golf. Players will be able to track their progress through:

Your Career

  • Games played
  • Wins
  • Best scores
  • Courses completed
  • Achievements

Tournaments

Competitive Mini Golf is coming. The new tournament system will allow players to compete in organized events, possibly featuring:

Tournament Play

  • Brackets
  • Leaderboards
  • Special rewards
  • Season events

Practice Mode

Players will soon be able to practice courses without needing a full match. Practice Mode will allow players to learn courses, improve shots, test strategies, and practice difficult holes.

MGLEPC says Mini Golf v2 is designed to transform the game from a simple attraction into a full competitive experience.
