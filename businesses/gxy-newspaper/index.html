MGLEPC has released a new sneak peek into two upcoming MiniGame Land attractions: Mini Golf and BreachPoint. Mini Golf — the first major build of the Expansion Project — brings a slower, skill-based game, while BreachPoint appears to be one of the most mysterious and intense games in the lineup, with a tactical, mission-style feel. The sneak peek has created major excitement ahead of the official expansion launch.
