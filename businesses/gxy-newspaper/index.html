Second Factory Completed in TCN

GXY Logistics has officially completed the building for its Second Factory in The City North. The factory is now set to become an XP / Armory Factory — producing, storing, and distributing equipment for future city projects, Nether operations, MiniGame Land systems, and possible off-world missions. With this factory now built, TCN is becoming one of the most important production districts in the world.
