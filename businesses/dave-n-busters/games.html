TNT Run Deluxe

Run across disappearing floors — don't stop or you'll fall into the lava.
Live · v1.0
The TNT Run Deluxe arena, now open in MiniGame Land

🎮 How to Play

Classic step-off floor decay: when you step on a block, the floor beneath you disappears about 0.4 seconds later — so keep moving. Fall through all three layers (Y -46, Y -52, Y -57) and you hit the lava pit. Drop to Y -59 or below and you're eliminated into spectator mode. The last player standing wins. Open the menu in-game with /function tntrun:menu.

🎯 Modes & Features

3 floor layers Auto winner & draw detection Solo Practice mode Spectator after elimination TNTWins leaderboard Best survival time tracking Arcade ticket rewards Auto-rebuilding floor Bossbars, titles & sound cues Tunable difficulty

🎟️ Ticket Rewards

Winner earns 2 tickets, eliminated players earn 1 ticket — all connected to the Dave n' Busters arcade ticket system. Match safety limit defaults to 300 seconds; if it's reached, all survivors share the win.
