The Future of MiniGame Land

Dave n' Busters is growing from an arcade and restaurant into a full entertainment hub — new games, updated classics, tickets, rewards, leaderboards, and challenges. This is the next era of MiniGame Land.

MiniGame Land is getting one of its biggest expansions yet. Below is the full planned lineup: ten new games, major updates to every current game, an upgraded arcade hub, and a unified ticket-and-prize system. Plans are subject to change as the expansion is built.
