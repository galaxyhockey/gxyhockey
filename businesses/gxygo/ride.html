🎟️ Fares & How to Ride

Everything you need to ride GxyGo across Biking People World — from boarding to transfers.

🚉 How to Ride

  1. Head to any GxyGo station — check the Live Board to see which trains are running.
  2. Find your line and direction on the platform signs. Lines are color-coded (see the fare card below).
  3. Wait behind the platform line. Trains stop automatically at each station on their schedule.
  4. Board when the doors open and grab a seat — Nether Line riders, mind the portal transition!
  5. Watch the next-stop display. The train announces each station as it arrives.
  6. Step off at your stop. Transfer to another line at any shared station (like Skyline Street).

ℹ️ Good to Know

Trains run on Create mod contraptions and follow set schedules — they may pause, reverse, or run cyclically. The Live Board shows real-time speed, length, and position for every train, including ones spanning the Nether portal. If a train shows as derailed, staff have been notified.
